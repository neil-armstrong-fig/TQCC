One of our classic annual rides! The St Johns Lighthouse challenge.

## The Route

- Start from Belfast
- Head south to **St Johns Lighthouse** near Kilough
- Return to Belfast

This challenging route covers approximately 90 miles with 3,760 feet of elevation gain. The varied terrain includes numerous segments with both climbs and descents, making it a substantial test of fitness and endurance.

## What to Expect

- Approximately 90 miles round trip
- 3,760 feet of elevation gain
- Varied terrain with multiple climbs
- Groups may combine based on pace and fitness
- A real sense of achievement at the end!

Make sure you're well-prepared with food, water, and spares for the journey.
