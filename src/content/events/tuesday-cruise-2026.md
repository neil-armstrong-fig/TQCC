Join us for the Easter Tuesday Cruise - a spectacular 71.7-mile ride through Northern Ireland!

**Route Details:**
- **Distance:** 71.7 miles
- **Elevation Gain:** 2,930 feet
- **Terrain:** Mixed terrain with 48 segments including notable climbs and scenic roads

**Key Areas:**
The route takes you through beautiful countryside including areas around Saintfield, Ballyhalbert, Portavogie, and Scrabo, featuring both challenging climbs and rewarding descents.

**Notable Climbs:**
- Cairn Point Climb (0.46 mi, 430 ft elevation gain)
- Listooder Hill (3.05 mi)
- Scrabo Loop (2.73 mi, 282 ft elevation gain)

Route created by Colin Roberts. This is a challenging ride suitable for experienced cyclists looking for a great Easter Tuesday adventure!
