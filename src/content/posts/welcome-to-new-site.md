Welcome to the brand new Titanic Quarter Cycling Club website!

We've completely rebuilt our online home from the ground up to make it easier for you to find ride information, keep up with club news, and stay connected with fellow members.

## What's New

- **Modern design** — Clean, fast, and works great on your phone
- **Easy ride info** — Find our weekly ride schedule and upcoming events at a glance
- **Club news** — Regular updates, race reports, and ride stories
- **Social feeds** — See our latest Instagram posts and Strava activities right here
- **Better contact** — Get in touch with us easily through the new contact form

## Stay Connected

Make sure to follow us on social media and join our Strava club to stay in the loop. We'll be posting regular updates here on the blog, so check back often!

See you on the road.
